Student flow

  1. Login and update your group name and student names.
  2. Review the 100-keyword database, audience hint, and landing pages.
  3. Plan keyword rows with your own headline, description, and budget allocation.
  4. If trial mode is active, test first. When admin turns it off, submit the official round.
  5. After an official round is submitted, click Next Round when available.
